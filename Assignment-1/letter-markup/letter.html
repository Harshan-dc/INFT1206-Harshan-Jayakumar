Exotic Dance Research

Regarding your question on tribal dances, I did study them during my post-doctorate. My favorites include:

Polynesian Chicken Dance
A culturally influential dance from 300 BCE, performed in circles to promote livestock fertility.
Icelandic Brownian Shuffle
Pre-fire, Icelanders practiced a floor circle shuffle with subtle, rapid movements. This may have inspired modern dance styles like Twerking.
Arctic Robot Dance
Misinterpreted by explorers in the 1960s as robotic movement, but actually a response to extreme cold conditions.
